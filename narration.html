Practice Narration

During the guest lecture, I learned that the crucial role of a designer is to construct a narrative and provide support for the thesis. This can be quite challenging at times, as various stakeholders may have different perspectives on digital products. The key skill for designers is to effectively communicate and convey their ideas to the intended audience.

Source: Heftmann, Jacob. “WHAT IS PRODUCT DESIGN?” Lecture, September 6, 2023.
